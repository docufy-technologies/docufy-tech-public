# Docufy Tech — Website Project Brief

## Company Overview

Docufy Tech (`tech.docufybd.com`) is the web development services subsidiary of Docufy, a documentation services company based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Sneha Salam is Founder & CEO. The subsidiary is not yet fully operational but is establishing formal business presence through this website.

---

## Project Objective

Build a static, multi-page marketing website that serves as proof of business existence, communicates professionalism and credibility to international enterprise clients, and drives inbound meeting bookings through a Calendly link. There is no e-commerce or order-placing system. The site is informational and conversion-focused.

---

## Target Audience

International businesses and enterprise clients looking for a reliable tech services partner for web application development.

---

## Core Value Propositions to Communicate

Two trust pillars must be felt throughout the entire site, not just stated:

1. **Delivery integrity** — what is promised is exactly what gets built.
2. **Research-driven execution** — every project is deeply researched and engineered toward specific, measurable outcomes.

---

## Conversion Mechanism

Single CTA across the site: book a meeting via Calendly. Implemented as an anchor link. No embedded widget complexity.

## Design Direction

- **Theme:** Dark
- **Typography:** Sora (headings) + Cabinet Grotesk (body)
- **Feel:** Minimalistic, modern, trending — not corporate-generic
- **Principle:** White space is a design element, not wasted space.
